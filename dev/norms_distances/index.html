    Norms and Distances

    Distances and norms

    We provide functions for computing norms and distances.

    HomotopyContinuation.euclidean_distance — Function.
    euclidean_distance(u, v)

    Compute ||u-v||₂.

    HomotopyContinuation.euclidean_norm — Function.
    euclidean_norm(u)

    Compute ||u||₂.

    HomotopyContinuation.infinity_distance — Function.
    infinity_distance(u, v)

    Compute the ∞-norm of u-v.

    HomotopyContinuation.infinity_norm — Function.
    infinity_norm(z)

    Compute the ∞-norm of z. If z is a complex vector this is more efficient than norm(z, Inf).

    infinity_norm(z₁, z₂)

    Compute the ∞-norm of z₁-z₂.

    HomotopyContinuation.fubini_study — Function.
    fubini_study(x::PVector, y::PVector)

    Computes the Fubini-Study distance between x and y.
